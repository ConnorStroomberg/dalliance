Keyboard shortcuts

Browser Navigation
Mouse must be within browser window to navigate; click on track label if focus has been lost.
← or → Scroll left or right (increasing or decreasing genomic coordinates)
ctrl ← or ctrl → Search left or right (next feature above threashold in selected track)
Space bar Toggle zoom to base pair coordinates and back
Track Navigation
Highlighted track label is selected track
↑ or ↓ Navigate up or down to select a track
shift ↑ or shift ↓ Expand or contract selected track
t For tracks with ⊕ toogle expansion (genes ↔ transcripts)
